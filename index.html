ABOUT THE INDIAN CELEBRATION

Holi is a spring festival that is celebrated throughout India and Nepal. The festival marks the beginning of the Hindu New Year, and it is a time for people to come together and celebrate. Holi is also known as the ‘Festival of Colours’, and people of all ages celebrate it.

During the festival, people throw coloured powder and water at each other, and the streets are filled with colour. Holi is a time for joy and celebration, and it is an incredible opportunity to experience Indian culture firsthand – it’s one of the best festivals in India.
